Rates

Full Day - Mature Dog $25
Your mature dog deserves a day full of comfort, enrichment, and gentle play. Our full-day care includes low-impact activities to suit your senior pet’s energy levels, along with plenty of rest breaks, cozy lounging spaces, and attentive care. We ensure that older dogs get the perfect balance of socialization and relaxation.
Full Day - Puppy $30
Puppies need playtime, learning, and naps! Our full-day care for puppies is designed to keep them active, stimulated, and happy. With supervised play, basic training exercises, and quiet time for naps, your puppy will enjoy a balanced day of fun and growth in a safe, nurturing environment.
Full Day - Cat $20
Your feline friend will experience a stress-free, enriching day in our full-day cat care service. With cozy resting areas, interactive toys, and plenty of quiet time, we provide the perfect space for your cat to explore, play, and relax. Our team ensures that every cat enjoys a calm and secure environment throughout the day.

Every month, Purrfect Pet Care offers an exclusive day of pampering, play, and socialization designed to keep your furry friend happy and healthy. Along with professional grooming and interactive playdates, our certified trainers ensure that every activity is safe and tailored to your pet’s needs.
